A numbered kicker + Fraunces title + body — the manifesto pillars and "why now" cards. Drop several into a hairline grid for the pillar wall.

```jsx
<FeatureCard kicker="01 — Compliance" title="Tracciabilità che tiene.">
  GMP, Annex 11: i requisiti diventano ogni anno più stringenti. La tracciabilità manuale non scala.
</FeatureCard>
```

Pair with `Card` (for boxed team members) or place bare inside a `.pillars` hairline grid. Kicker accepts numbers or labels.
