Generic bordered surface — the base box for form panels, team members and boxed content.

```jsx
<Card hover>
  <Eyebrow>Clinical</Eyebrow>
  <h4>Farmacisti ospedalieri</h4>
  <p>Capiamo i processi perché abbiamo lavorato su quei banchi.</p>
</Card>
```

Flags: `hover` (tint + border), `lift` (shadow lift), `flat` (border only, no fill). Theme-aware.
