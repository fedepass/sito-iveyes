A roadmap row — Fraunces year beside a main line + subtext. Wrap several in a `<ul>` for the company timeline; rows carry their own bottom hairline and hover tint.

```jsx
<ul style={{listStyle:'none'}}>
  <TimelineItem year="2026" title="Fondazione Hexaros e primo pilota ospedaliero">
    Avvio del programma pilota con i primi partner ospedalieri.
  </TimelineItem>
  <TimelineItem year="2027 →" title="Prima release commerciale e nuovi moduli" />
</ul>
```
