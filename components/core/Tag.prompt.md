Mono uppercase pill chip for statuses, product parentage and roadmap markers.

```jsx
<Tag tone="teal">Un prodotto Hexaros</Tag>
<Tag tone="gold">Primo prodotto · Disponibile</Tag>
<Tag tone="muted" dashed>Q3 2026 — In sviluppo</Tag>
```

Tones: `gold` (theme accent — gold on dark, clinical teal-blue on light) · `teal` · `success` (validation green) · `muted` · `solid`. Set `dashed` for roadmap / coming-soon framing.
