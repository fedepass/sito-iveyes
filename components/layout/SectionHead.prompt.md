The recurring two-column section header: a mono eyebrow on the left, a large Fraunces heading on the right. Wrap a phrase in `<em>` for the signature accent italic emphasis.

```jsx
<SectionHead eyebrow="Perché ora">
  Tre pressioni convergenti, <em>una risposta sola.</em>
</SectionHead>
```

Collapses to a single column under 860px. Pass `lede` for a supporting paragraph.
